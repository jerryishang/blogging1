Hello, my name is Yisheng (Jerry) Zhang. I am more often called by my first name Jerry, but I prefer Yisheng because Jerry is too common of a name. I am 19 years old, and I live in the North York area of the Toronto area.
I tend to spend most of my free time and school time on my own. I hardly ever hang out with friends, and I am the kind of guy you see sitting in an empty row in the classroom.
Nevertheless, I strongly see the power in group work, and I am eager to have more study partners, and join more study groups. I am even willing to create a side project with a group of people if I ever have time. Trying to self-learn a whole new topic is extremely difficult, but it is often what students face in during university.
I am currently a 2a Electrical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I am looking for a co-op placement for the winter 2016 term.
Unlike my friends who have grown up to be musicians or entrepreneurs, I never stayed for years in whatever class I took. I wasted a lot of my free summers and vacations watching movies, fooling around on the internet, or playing video games. Those are pretty wasteful habits.
